Platinum Group Elements and Rare Earth Element Mines Across Australia

According to Geoscience Australia March 2024 - Serena Sivaraj 32494343

Australia's wealth in natural resources has established it as a global leader in mining, significantly contributing to both the national economy and international markets. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of mining activities across the country, highlighting key aspects of production and the geographic distribution of resources.

The first visualization on the dashboard presents a detailed comparison of Platinum Group Elements (PGE) and Rare Earth Elements (REE) production across different states. By displaying this data side by side, users can easily discern which states lead in the extraction of these vital resources. PGE, which includes metals like platinum, palladium, and rhodium, plays a crucial role in various industries, from automotive catalysis to electronics. Monitoring PGE production helps in understanding market trends and future demands, as these elements are essential for green technologies, including electric vehicles and renewable energy systems.

The bar chart to the left highlights key metrics related to the mining operations across various regions. This provides further insights into the volume of extraction for critical resources such as Platinum Group Elements (PGE) and Rare Earth Elements (REE).

Additional focus is placed on the economic impact these operations have on the Australian economy, especially in states like Western Australia, Queensland, and New South Wales where mining is a significant part of regional development.

Mining plays a crucial role in Australia's economy, contributing significantly to export revenues. With resources spread across various states, it's essential to monitor and understand the distribution of mining operations and the commodities being extracted. This dashboard serves as a useful tool for gaining insights into these operations.
